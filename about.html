Karla Bear
Karla Bear is your typical Aussie, blonde bombshell... think Kylie Minogue, but with height. Originally born in the most recent cast of KINKY BOOTS- The Musical, this 7ft live singing and dancing Glamazon has legs for DAYS and a vocal range to match. In her muggle form, she has been seen all around the world in Kinky Boots, Priscilla Queen of the Desert and WICKED and, in 2019, she won Drag Idol UK- a National Drag competition giving her the chance to tour the country performing in the most prestigious LGBTI+ venues the UK has to offer. Belting out anything from Musical Theatre to Top of the Pops, Whitney Houston to Ariana, and everything in-between, the focus isn't all Karla will be stealing... LOCK UP YOUR HUSBANDS!
Portia De Fosse
Portia De Fosse is the creation of Steven. As a trained performer Steven has worked in various productions in the West End. Some of Stevens Theatre Credits include Mel Brookes ‘The Producers’ (Theatre Royal Drury Lane), ‘La Cage Aux Folles’ (Menier Chocolate Factory), and the original cast of ‘Priscilla Queen of The Desert The Musical’ (The Palace Theatre) where he created the role of Farrah & Young Bernadette. Portia has been extremely lucky to work with such amazing venues up and down the UK and abroad. She holds residences at Soho House, & Old Compton Brasserie. Most recently Portia has just come back from Ibiza where she has been performing at iconic venues such as the infamous Pikes Hotel and Wikiwoo to name a few. This year has also seen her work for big name brands such as The Perfume Shop, Benefit Makeup, and the very first Rupauls Drag Con UK.
Velma Celli
Known as “The UK’s Queen of live vocal drag”, Velma has been charming audiences across the globe for the past 10 years. The alter ego of West End star Ian Stroughair who has appeared in hit musicals Cats, Fame, Chicago and Rent, to which he received critical acclaim. Last year Velma’s “A Brief History of Drag” entertained audiences across the globe on a tour which included Australia (Best Cabaret Nomination in Perth), 54 Below in N.Y.C, a sell-out U.K tour ending in a smash hit run at the Edinburgh Fringe Festival. In 2015 Velma was invited to appear on BBC’s Eastenders for a very special guest episode playing herself, starred opposite Danny Dyer in the music short Nebraska, performed with pop icon Anastacia and West End and Broadway legends Ruthie Henshall, Kerry Ellis and Frances Ruffelle to name but a few. She can also be spotted in American comedy series Piss Off, I Love You and on the silver screen in The Night of The Lotus Her new show "Me & My Divas" won Best Cabaret at Perth fringeworld 2020 Last year she appeared in the Olivier award-winning circus show, La Soiree, fronted the McDonalds “we are awake” campaign, was nominated for a Great British Pantomine Award and even performed privately for royalty. The headline act for the BBC’s Best Of the Fringe closing night concert at the Edinburgh Fringe 2015 and the winner of the coveted Three Weeks Editors Award, a two-year long residency at The Hippodrome, London There isn’t much our Velma hasn’t done!
